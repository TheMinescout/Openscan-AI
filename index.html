How to Use

Quick Start Guide

1. Point & Align 🟦

Point camera at your document. The AI will find the edges and draw a Blue Box when locked on.

2. Auto-Capture 📸

Hold steady! The shutter ring will fill up. Once full, it captures automatically. Or tap the button to snap manually.

3. Edit & Filter 🎨

Tap the gallery preview. Use B&W for clear text, Rotate to fix orientation, or OCR to copy text.

4. Export PDF 📄

Tap the PDF button to combine all scans into one file. You can set a password in Settings.
